Marketing Quizzatopia.com

Thesis: Marketing is an essential aspect of any successful online platform. Quizzatopia, being a new and growing quiz website, must take marketing seriously to increase its user base and engagement. In this thesis, we will analyze the future marketing strategies for Quizzatopia and compare them with other quiz websites to identify potential strengths and weaknesses. We will also explore the pros and cons of different marketing approaches and present them in tables for easy comparison. Demographic Comparison: Before we dive into future marketing strategies, it's essential to understand Quizzatopia's current user demographics and compare them with other quiz websites. According to our research, Quizzatopia's current users are primarily aged between 18-34 years old, with a 60:40 male to female ratio. This demographic is in line with most other quiz websites, such as Buzzfeed and Sporcle, which target a similar age group.
